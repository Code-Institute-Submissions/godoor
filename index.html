Welcome to /godoor game traveller!
You are the lonely looking square on the left side of the screen...and you really REALLY need to get to the door!

There is just one problem. You see those red, angry looking squares jumping up-and-down like crazy? You don't like them, they don't like you. Avoid them at all cost!

Move forward by clicking/holding the mouse button anywhere in the blue square game area. Or touch the screen if you prefer mobile experience. Your call.

When you finally get to the door, guess what? Level Up! You will be moving faster but the red dudes will also get a kick!

Good luck and have fun!
